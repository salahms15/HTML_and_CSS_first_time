KAKAROT

Goku, also known as Kakarot, is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball series created by Akira Toriyama. He is a powerful Saiyan warrior who was sent to Earth as an infant before the destruction of his home planet, Planet Vegeta. Goku was discovered by Grandpa Gohan, who adopted him and named him Son Goku.
